FallGuard is developed by Neuron TS Labs. Your privacy is important to us. The app is designed to process information on your device whenever possible.

Permissions

PermissionPurpose
LocationInclude GPS coordinates in emergency SMS alerts.
SMSSend emergency SMS to trusted contacts.
PhoneCall emergency contacts if configured.
ContactsAllow you to choose emergency contacts.
NotificationsDisplay foreground service and emergency alerts.
Motion SensorsDetect falls using on-device sensors.

Background Location Disclosure

Background location is used only for the app's core safety feature. When a fall is detected and the alert is not dismissed, your location may be included in emergency SMS messages sent to your selected contacts.

Data Handling

  • No account registration.
  • Emergency contacts remain on your device.
  • Sensor data is processed locally.
  • No Neuron TS Labs server stores your emergency data.

Third-Party Services

If the app includes third-party services (such as advertising), those providers may process data according to their own privacy policies.

Your Choices

  • Grant or revoke permissions in Android Settings.
  • Edit or remove emergency contacts.
  • Disable monitoring whenever you choose.
  • Uninstall the app to remove local app data.
