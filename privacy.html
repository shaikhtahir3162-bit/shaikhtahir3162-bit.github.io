Privacy Policy for FallGuard

Last Updated: February 4, 2025

This Privacy Policy governs the "FallGuard" mobile application developed by NEURON TS LABS. By installing and using this App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. SENSITIVE DATA AND PERMISSIONS

Background Location Access: FallGuard collects precise location data even when the app is closed or not in use to include your location in emergency SMS alerts.

SMS and Call Processing: The App automatically sends SMS messages and may initiate calls to your emergency contacts when a fall is detected.

2. DATA MINIMIZATION

We follow a "Zero-Server" architecture. Your location and contacts are stored only on your local device and are never sent to our servers.
