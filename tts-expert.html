How TTS Expert Works

Text entered by you is processed on your device whenever offline voices are used. Exported audio files are saved only to the location you choose.

Data Collection

  • No account registration is required.
  • We do not collect or store the text you convert to speech on Neuron TS Labs servers.
  • Your exported audio remains under your control on your device.
  • Application settings are stored locally.

Third-Party Services

If the application includes advertising, analytics, or cloud-based voice providers, those services may process information according to their own privacy policies. Review those providers' policies where applicable.

Your Choices

  • Delete generated audio files whenever you choose.
  • Clear app data through Android Settings.
  • Manage permissions from Android Settings.
  • Uninstall the app to remove local app data.
