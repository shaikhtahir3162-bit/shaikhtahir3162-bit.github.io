Lite Photo Compressor is developed by Neuron TS Labs. The app is designed to compress your images while keeping your privacy a priority.

Permissions

PermissionPurpose
Photos / MediaSelect images that you choose to compress.
Storage (where applicable)Save compressed images on your device.

How Your Data Is Used

Images selected for compression are processed locally on your device. We do not upload your photos to Neuron TS Labs servers for compression.

Data Collection

  • No account registration is required.
  • We do not collect your personal photos for our own servers.
  • Your selected files remain under your control.
  • Compressed images are stored only where you choose to save them.

Third-Party Services

If the application includes third-party services such as advertising or analytics, those services may process data according to their own privacy policies.

Your Choices

  • Select only the images you want to compress.
  • Delete original or compressed files whenever you choose.
  • Revoke permissions through Android Settings.
  • Uninstall the app to remove its local data.
